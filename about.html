BUT I do have a wide range of interests that I am too lazy to list out right now, instead I will list out a receipe for cooking Sea Bass(or any fish).
  • Ingredients
    • Filet of Sea Bass (duh).
    • Two Bell Peppers (one yellow and one red).
    • Salt.
    • Olive Oil.
    • Rice Wine Vinegar.
    • Lemon.
    • Garlic.
    • Fresh Thyme and Basil.

  • Instruction for the sauce
    • Cut the peppers in strips.
    • Chop the garlic as well.
    • Olive oil in hot pan, and add the peppers in with garlic and a pinch of salt.
    • Stir until the peppers are soften up, then add basil and rice wine vinegar.
    • Add water and let it boil.
    • Take all the ingredients out and liquify them in a blender.

  • Instruction for the fish
    • Heat olive oil in a hot pan.
    • Add salt and thyme.
    • Be sure to hold the filet down to prevent curling.
    • Turn the filet when most of the meat has turned into a bright white color

  • To serve, simply pour the sauce into a plate. Add cooked Sea Bass, then top it off with olive oil and fresh lemon juice.
